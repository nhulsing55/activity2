Subaru WRX

Home Favorite Car


The Subaru WRX is one of the best bang for your buck sports car you can get on the market. The car has a history of being a rally car. The car is AWD, so it is a great car to use year-round. The car's MPG is 21 in the city and 27 on the highway. The WRX is loaded with 268 horsepower, making it a fun little car. The best part about the WRX is that it comes with a manual transmission.
